BILLING AND INVOICES

Invoice Requests:
- Invoices are generated automatically for all subscriptions
- To request a copy of an invoice, email finance@company.com
- Include your account number or order ID
- Invoices are typically sent within 24 hours
- Invoices are available in PDF format
- Historical invoices available for download from account portal
- Invoice numbers are unique and sequential
- Invoices include itemized charges, taxes, and payment terms
- Recurring invoices sent on billing date each month
- Pro forma invoices available for Enterprise customers

Invoice Details:
- Invoice number format: INV-YYYY-XXXXXX
- Payment terms: Net 30 for Enterprise, Due on receipt for others
- Late payment fee: 1.5% per month (Enterprise only)
- Invoice includes billing address, shipping address (if applicable)
- Tax information included based on customer location
- Currency displayed matches payment currency
- Multi-currency invoices supported for international customers

Payment Methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards (with credit card logo)
- Bank transfer (ACH) - US customers only
- Wire transfer (for amounts over $10,000 or Enterprise customers)
- Check payments (must be pre-approved, Enterprise only)
- PayPal (for small transactions)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay (mobile payments)
- Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum) - Enterprise only, requires approval

International Payment Methods:
- SEPA transfers (EU customers)
- SWIFT wire transfers (international)
- Local payment methods available in select countries
- Currency conversion handled automatically
- Exchange rates updated daily

To update your payment method:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Billing Settings
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
4. Enter new payment information
5. Verify payment method (small charge may be made and refunded)
6. Save changes
7. Set as default payment method if multiple methods on file

Payment Method Security:
- All payment information encrypted using PCI-DSS compliant systems
- Payment details never stored on our servers
- Tokenized payment processing for security
- 3D Secure authentication for additional security
- Fraud detection and monitoring systems active

BILLING CYCLE AND FREQUENCY

Billing Cycles:
- Monthly billing: Charges on same date each month
- Annual billing: Charges on anniversary date
- Quarterly billing: Available for Enterprise customers
- Custom billing cycles: Available for Enterprise with approval

Billing Date Changes:
- Can be changed once per year for free
- Additional changes incur $25 administrative fee
- Changes take effect on next billing cycle
- Prorated charges apply when changing billing date

Proration:
- Plan upgrades: Prorated charge for remainder of billing period
- Plan downgrades: Credit applied to next invoice
- Add-ons: Prorated based on activation date
- Cancellations: No refund for partial periods (unless within refund window)

REFUND POLICY

Refund Eligibility:
- Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase
- Subscription cancellations: refunds available within first 30 days only
- Refund processing time: 5-7 business days
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Partial refunds available for unused services
- Annual plan refunds: Prorated based on months used

To request a refund:
1. Contact customer support via email or phone
2. Provide order number and reason for refund
3. Support will review your request within 2 business days
4. If approved, refund will be processed within 5-7 business days
5. You'll receive email confirmation when refund is processed
6. Refund appears on statement within 1-2 billing cycles

Refund Exceptions:
- Services already used are not eligible
- Custom products/services may have different policies
- Refunds after 30 days require special approval
- Setup fees are non-refundable
- Third-party integrations and add-ons subject to vendor policies
- Promotional credits and discounts are non-refundable

Refund Methods:
- Credit card: Refunded to original card (5-7 business days)
- Bank transfer: Refunded to original account (7-10 business days)
- Wire transfer: Refunded to original account (10-14 business days)
- Check: Mailed to billing address (14-21 business days)

EXPENSE REPORTS

Expense Report Submission:
- Submit expense reports monthly by the 5th of the following month
- Use the company expense reporting system (accessible via employee portal)
- Attach all receipts (required for expenses over $25)
- Get manager approval before submission
- Late submissions may delay reimbursement
- Reports can be submitted via mobile app or web portal
- Receipts can be uploaded as photos or PDFs
- Multiple currencies supported with automatic conversion

Reimbursable Expenses:
- Business travel (flights, hotels, meals, ground transportation)
- Client entertainment (meals, events, tickets)
- Office supplies and equipment
- Professional development courses and certifications
- Business-related software subscriptions
- Internet and phone expenses (for remote workers)
- Home office equipment (with manager approval)
- Conference and event registration fees
- Business meals (with client or team members)
- Parking and tolls (business-related)

Non-Reimbursable Expenses:
- Personal expenses
- Alcohol (unless client entertainment, requires approval)
- Personal travel upgrades
- Expenses without receipts (over $25)
- Traffic violations and fines
- Personal entertainment
- Gifts over $50 (requires special approval)
- Political contributions
- Personal insurance premiums

Expense Limits:
- Meals: $75 per person per day (domestic), $100 (international)
- Hotels: Reasonable rates based on location
- Flights: Economy class standard, business class requires approval
- Car rentals: Standard size, upgrades require approval
- Client entertainment: $200 per event, higher amounts require approval

Reimbursement Timeline:
- Reports submitted by the 5th: paid by the 20th
- Late submissions: paid in the following cycle
- Direct deposit to your bank account
- Payment notifications sent via email
- Reimbursement appears in account within 2-3 business days of payment

PAYMENT FAILURES AND RESOLUTION

If your payment fails:
1. You'll receive an email notification immediately
2. We'll attempt to charge again after 3 days
3. Second retry attempt after 6 days
4. Final retry attempt after 9 days
5. You have a 7-day grace period to update payment
6. Account will be suspended if payment not resolved after 7 days
7. To reactivate: update payment method and account restores immediately
8. Data retention: 30 days after suspension before deletion

Common Payment Failure Reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Expired credit card
- Card declined by bank
- Incorrect billing address
- Card limit reached
- Bank security hold
- International transaction blocked

To resolve payment issues:
- Update payment method in account settings
- Contact finance@company.com for assistance
- Check with your bank for any holds or restrictions
- Verify billing address matches card statement
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact support for payment plan options (Enterprise only)

Payment Failure Notifications:
- Email sent immediately upon failure
- Reminder emails sent before retry attempts
- Final warning email sent before suspension
- SMS notifications available (opt-in required)
- In-app notifications for active users

ACCOUNT SUSPENSION AND REACTIVATION

Suspended Account Status:
- Access to services restricted
- Data retained for 30 days
- Email notifications continue
- Can still access billing portal
- No new charges during suspension

Reactivation Process:
1. Update payment method in account settings
2. Payment will be automatically processed
3. Account restored immediately upon successful payment
4. All data and settings preserved
5. Email confirmation sent upon reactivation

Data Recovery:
- Data available for 30 days after suspension
- After 30 days, data may be permanently deleted
- Backup restoration available for Enterprise customers
- Contact support immediately if you need data recovery

BILLING DISPUTES AND RESOLUTION

Disputing a Charge:
1. Contact finance@company.com within 60 days
2. Provide invoice number and reason for dispute
3. Include any supporting documentation
4. Finance team will investigate within 5 business days
5. Resolution communicated via email
6. If dispute is valid, adjustment made to account

Chargeback Process:
- Contact us before initiating chargeback
- We can resolve most issues without chargeback
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension
- We'll work with you to resolve the issue
- Documentation provided to payment processor if needed
