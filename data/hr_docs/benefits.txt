HEALTH INSURANCE BENEFITS

Our company offers comprehensive health insurance benefits to all full-time employees.

Medical Insurance:
- Coverage begins on the first day of employment
- We offer three plan options: Basic, Standard, and Premium
- The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 60% for dependents
- Deductibles range from $500 (Premium) to $2,000 (Basic)
- Annual out-of-pocket maximums range from $3,000 to $6,000
- Coverage includes preventive care, emergency services, hospitalization, prescription drugs
- Mental health and substance abuse treatment covered
- Maternity and newborn care included
- Pediatric services covered
- Network includes 95% of providers nationwide
- Out-of-network coverage available (higher cost)

Plan Comparison:
- Basic Plan: $500 deductible, $3,000 OOP max, 80/20 coinsurance
- Standard Plan: $1,000 deductible, $4,500 OOP max, 85/15 coinsurance
- Premium Plan: $2,000 deductible, $6,000 OOP max, 90/10 coinsurance

Open Enrollment:
- Annual open enrollment period: November 1-15
- Changes take effect January 1
- Can change plans, add/drop dependents during open enrollment
- Qualifying life events allow mid-year changes
- HR available for enrollment assistance

Dental Insurance:
- Preventive care is covered at 100% (cleanings, exams, X-rays)
- Basic procedures covered at 80% (fillings, extractions)
- Major procedures covered at 50% (crowns, bridges, root canals)
- Orthodontia covered at 50% up to $2,000 lifetime maximum
- Annual maximum benefit of $1,500 per person
- No deductible for preventive care
- $50 deductible for basic procedures, $100 for major
- Network includes 90% of dentists nationwide
- Annual cleanings: 2 per year covered at 100%

Vision Insurance:
- Annual eye exam covered at 100%
- Eyeglasses or contact lenses covered up to $200 per year
- Frames: $150 allowance every 2 years
- Lenses: Standard lenses covered, upgrades available
- Contact lenses: $200 allowance per year (alternative to glasses)
- Discounts on LASIK surgery available (20% off)
- Network includes major vision centers
- Online ordering available through insurance portal

- Target-date funds available

- Automatic enrollment at 3% (can opt out)
- Annual increase program: Auto-increase 1% per year (can opt out)

Retirement Planning:

- Online retirement calculator and tools
- Educational workshops quarterly
- One-on-one consultations with financial advisors
- Retirement readiness assessments

LIFE INSURANCE AND DISABILITY

Life Insurance:
- Basic life insurance: 1x annual salary (company paid)
- Additional coverage available: Up to 5x salary (employee paid)
- Spouse coverage: Up to $250,000 available
- Dependent children coverage: $10,000 per child
- Accidental death and dismemberment (AD&D): Included
- Premiums based on age and coverage amount
- Beneficiary designation required
- Coverage continues after retirement (reduced amount)

Short-Term Disability:
- Coverage: 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks
- Elimination period: 7 days
- Company paid for all employees
- Covers illness, injury, pregnancy
- Job protection during disability period

Long-Term Disability:
- Coverage: 60% of salary up to $10,000/month
- Elimination period: 90 days
- Company paid for all employees
- Coverage until age 65 or return to work
- Own occupation definition for first 2 years
- Cost of living adjustments available

VACATION AND LEAVE POLICIES

Vacation Days:
- Full-time employees accrue 15 vacation days per year
- Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.25 days per month
- Maximum carryover of 5 days to the next year
- Unused days beyond carryover limit are forfeited
- Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Manager approval required for all vacation requests
- Blackout periods may apply (check with manager)
- Vacation can be taken in half-day increments
- Vacation accrual increases with tenure:
  - Years 1-3: 15 days
  - Years 4-6: 20 days
  - Years 7-10: 25 days
  - Years 11+: 30 days

Sick Leave:
- 10 paid sick days per year
- Unused sick days do not carry over
- Sick leave can be used for personal illness or family member care
- No doctor's note required for 3 days or less
- Doctor's note required for 4+ consecutive days
- Can be used for medical appointments
- Can be used for mental health days
- Accrues at 0.83 days per month

Personal Days:
- 3 personal days per year
- Can be used for any reason
- Do not require advance notice (within reason)
- Do not carry over to next year
- Use it or lose it policy

Maternity/Paternity Leave:
- 12 weeks of paid maternity leave
- 6 weeks of paid paternity leave
- Additional unpaid leave available under FMLA (up to 12 weeks total)
- Leave can begin up to 2 weeks before due date
- Adoption leave: 6 weeks paid
- Surrogacy leave: 6 weeks paid
- Leave runs concurrently with FMLA
- Health insurance continues during leave
- Job protection guaranteed
- Return to work transition program available

Bereavement Leave:
- 3 days paid for immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling)
- 1 day paid for extended family (grandparent, aunt, uncle, cousin)
- Additional unpaid leave available with manager approval
- No limit on bereavement leave for immediate family

Jury Duty:
- Paid time off for jury duty
- Provide jury summons to HR
- Difference between jury pay and regular salary paid by company
- No limit on duration

Military Leave:
- Unpaid leave for military service
- Job protection under USERRA
- Health benefits continue for up to 24 months
- Reinstatement rights protected
